# **How Learning Paths Work**

Each role in the IT Department has a structured learning path covering mandatory training, recommended upskilling, and long-term certification goals. All mandatory items are assigned automatically in NexoraLearn on your start date. Recommended items are self-enrol.

| **NexoraLearn** | learn.nexoraglobal.com — the LMS for all mandatory and optional training |
| --- | --- |
| **Learning stipend** | USD 2,000/year for external courses, conferences, certifications — request in Workday > My Learning > External Development |
| **Study leave** | Up to 5 days/year for approved external exams — request in Workday > Time Off > Study Leave |
| **Certification funding** | Exam fees reimbursed on first pass; second attempt at employee expense |
