# **Overview**

Nexora's performance cycle is designed to be continuous — not a once-a-year event. It combines structured formal reviews with ongoing feedback and real-time recognition.

| **Phase** | **When** | **What Happens** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Goal Setting** | January (Weeks 1–3) | Employee and manager agree OKRs in Workday. Goals are aligned to team and company OKRs. Completed by Jan 31. |
| **Mid-Year Check-In** | June | Informal 1:1 conversation. Goals reviewed, updated if needed. No formal rating. Documented in Workday. |
| **Year-End Review** | November–December | Formal self-assessment, manager assessment, and peer feedback via Workday. Calibration sessions held. |
| **Rating Communicated** | January (following year) | Manager shares rating and narrative in 1:1. Compensation decisions communicated separately. |
| **Compensation Review** | January–February | Pay increases, bonuses, and equity grants communicated. Effective March 1 payroll. |

# **The Rating Scale**

| **Rating** | **Label** | **What It Means** | **Approximate Distribution** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **5** | Exceptional | Consistently exceeds all goals; role model for the team and values; demonstrably raised the bar | ~5% |
| **4** | Exceeds Expectations | Regularly exceeds key objectives with strong impact; highly valued contributor | ~20% |
| **3** | Meets Expectations | Fully delivers on goals; demonstrates Nexora values consistently; solid contributor | ~60% |
| **2** | Developing | Partially meets goals; improvement in specific areas needed; active development plan in place | ~12% |
| **1** | Below Expectations | Significant performance gaps; formal Performance Improvement Plan required | ~3% |

## **Calibration**

After manager ratings are submitted, each department holds a calibration session — a cross-manager discussion to ensure ratings are consistent and fair across teams. The People Analytics team provides benchmarking data. Calibration is facilitated by the HR Business Partner.

# **Continuous Feedback**

## **How to Give Feedback**

- Use the Feedback module in Workday to send structured feedback to any colleague at any time

- Feedback prompts: 'What went well?', 'What could be even better?', 'What should they keep doing?'

- Feedback is visible to the recipient and their manager — there is no anonymous peer feedback at Nexora

## **NexoraShoutout — Recognition Programme**

NexoraShoutout is our peer recognition platform (integrated into Slack). Anyone can give a Shoutout tied to one of our five values. Shoutouts are visible company-wide, and can be redeemed for rewards via the Shoutout portal.

## **Manager 1:1 Cadence**

| **Frequency** | Minimum bi-weekly for all employees; weekly for new joiners in first 90 days |
| --- | --- |
| **Format** | No fixed agenda — employee owns the agenda; manager listens and coaches |
| **Documentation** | Optional but encouraged — key decisions and actions noted in Workday |
| **First 1:1** | Should occur within the new joiner's first 3 days |
