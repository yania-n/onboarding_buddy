# **IT All-Hands**

| **Frequency** | Monthly |
| --- | --- |
| **Duration** | 60 minutes |
| **Owner** | IT Director |
| **Audience** | All IT staff globally — virtual + in-person Austin |

## **Agenda Template**

- IT Director opening — company context, key messages (10 min)

- Highlights from each sub-team — 3 min per team lead: one win, one challenge (20 min)

- Spotlight — one team presents a deep dive on something they built or learned (15 min)

- Metrics dashboard review — SLO performance, incident count, cost, backlog health (10 min)

- Q&A — open floor (5 min)

## **Notes**

Slides prepared by IT Director and team leads. Recording posted to Confluence: IT Department > All-Hands Recordings within 24h.

# **Architecture Review Board (ARB)**

| **Frequency** | Bi-weekly, Thursday |
| --- | --- |
| **Duration** | 60 minutes |
| **Owner** | Lead Architect (TCP Team) |
| **Audience** | Senior engineers, TPOs, IT Director (standing invite) |

## **Agenda Template**

- Review of previous ARB actions — are items from last session closed? (5 min)

- Proposed design reviews — each presenter has 15 min: 10 min presentation, 5 min Q&A (up to 3 per session)

- Standards and principles — any proposed changes to IT architecture standards (10 min)

- Emerging topics — technology radar updates, new vendor evaluations (5 min)

## **How to Submit a Design for Review**

Create a design document in Confluence (IT Department > Architecture > Proposed Designs) using the ARB Design Template. Submit to the Lead Architect at least 5 business days before the ARB you wish to present at. Designs not submitted in time will be scheduled for the following session.

# **Cloud Cost Review**

| **Frequency** | Monthly, first Thursday |
| --- | --- |
| **Duration** | 60 minutes |
| **Owner** | FinOps Analyst |
| **Audience** | Cloud Ops Lead, IT Director, CTO, Finance BP |

## **Agenda Template**

- Month-in-review — total cloud spend vs budget; YoY comparison (10 min)

- Spend by team / account / service — anomalies and explanations (15 min)

- Forecast — next 3 months projection vs budget (10 min)

- Optimisation actions — status of current optimisation initiatives; new recommendations (15 min)

- Decisions needed — any budget increase requests, RI/Savings Plan approvals (10 min)

## **Pre-work**

FinOps Analyst sends a pre-read cost report (Power BI export) to all attendees 48 hours before the meeting.

# **Security Posture Review**

| **Frequency** | Monthly, third Tuesday |
| --- | --- |
| **Duration** | 45 minutes |
| **Owner** | Cloud Security Lead |
| **Audience** | Cloud Ops Lead, IT Director, Cybersecurity team rep |

## **Agenda Template**

- CSPM dashboard review — current critical and high findings (10 min)

- Remediation progress — status of findings from last month (10 min)

- New findings — significant new issues discovered this month and response plan (10 min)

- Compliance status — SOC 2 / ISO 27001 control status; upcoming audit preparation (10 min)

- Decisions needed — any exception requests, risk acceptances (5 min)

# **Sprint Review — Analytics Platform**

| **Frequency** | Every 2 weeks, Friday of sprint end |
| --- | --- |
| **Duration** | 45 minutes |
| **Owner** | TPO (facilitator) + Dev Lead (technical co-presenter) |
| **Audience** | Functional PO, Business Analysts, Business Consultants, IT Director (standing invite) |

## **Agenda Template**

- Sprint goal recap — what did we commit to? (2 min)

- Demos — working software only; TPO introduces each story, developer demos (25 min)

- What we didn't complete — brief explanation; what happens to the items (5 min)

- Metrics — velocity, bug count, SLO performance (5 min)

- Next sprint preview — top priorities for next sprint (5 min)

- Q&A and feedback (3 min)

## **Rules**

- Demo only working software — never demo code that hasn't passed DoD

- No slides for demos — use the actual product in staging

- Incomplete stories are returned to backlog without ceremony
