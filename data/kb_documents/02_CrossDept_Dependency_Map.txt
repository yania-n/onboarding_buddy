# **About This Map**

This document maps the key dependencies, handoffs, and collaboration points between Nexora's departments. It is used by the onboarding system to answer questions like 'who does IT work with?' and 'where do my requests come from?'

## **Reading This Map**

- From → To: the direction of a primary dependency or service

- Type: the nature of the relationship (Data, Service, Approval, Collaboration)

- Frequency: how often the interaction typically occurs

- Key contact: the role that manages the relationship on each side
