# **Universal IT Setup — All IT Staff**

Complete these steps on Day 1 before anything else. If any step fails, contact IT Helpdesk (helpdesk@nexoraglobal.com or ext. 5000) immediately.

## **Hardware ****&**** Accounts**

- Collect or power on your laptop — confirm it is registered in Jamf/Intune (your manager can check)

- Sign in to your Nexora Microsoft account (firstname.lastname@nexoraglobal.com)

- Set up Microsoft Authenticator MFA on your phone — required before any other system access

- Open 1Password — accept the invite email and set your master password (store it safely — not in 1Password itself)

- Open Slack — sign in with your Nexora Microsoft SSO. Join your team channels (list below per role)

- Open Outlook — confirm your email is working and check your welcome email from IT

- Connect to the Nexora VPN (GlobalProtect) — installer in Self Service (Mac) or Software Centre (Windows)

## **Portal Access**

- Log into ServiceNow (it.nexoraglobal.com) — confirm you can see your open access requests

- Log into Workday (workday.nexoraglobal.com) — confirm your employment details, start benefits enrolment

- Log into NexoraLearn (learn.nexoraglobal.com) — confirm your mandatory training modules are assigned

- Log into NexoraConnect (connect.nexoraglobal.com) — bookmark the IT Department page
