# **Understanding Your Payslip**

| **Pay date** | Last business day of each month — paid by direct bank deposit |
| --- | --- |
| **Payslip access** | Workday > Pay > Payslips — available on pay date each month |
| **Gross pay** | Your base salary divided by 12 (monthly) or 26 (bi-weekly where applicable) |
| **Deductions** | Tax withholding, benefits premiums, pension/401k contributions, FSA contributions |
| **Net pay** | The amount deposited to your bank account after all deductions |
| **P60 / W-2** | Annual tax summary issued by 31 January (USA) or by 31 May (UK) via Workday |

## **Banking Details**

Submit your bank account details in Workday within your first 3 days to ensure your first payslip is paid on time. Navigate to: Workday > Pay > Payment Elections > Add. Nexora does not issue paper cheques.

# **Submitting Expenses**

## **The Expense Process**

- Incur a business expense (pre-approved by manager where required)

- Keep your receipt — photograph it on the day

- Log into Concur at travel.nexoraglobal.com

- Create an Expense Report: Expense > Create Report

- Add expense lines, upload receipt photos, select expense category and cost centre

- Submit for manager approval — manager must approve within 5 business days

- Finance processes approved reports within 5 business days

- Reimbursement arrives with your next payroll or as a separate bank transfer

## **Expense Categories ****&**** Required Information**

| **Category** | **Receipt Required?** | **Additional Info Required** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Meals — business** | Yes (> USD 25) | Names of attendees; business purpose |
| **Travel — flights** | Yes | Booking confirmation; class of travel |
| **Travel — hotels** | Yes | Nightly rate must be within policy limits |
| **Travel — ground** | Yes (> USD 25) | Start/end location; business purpose |
| **Professional development** | Yes | Name of course/event; learning objective |
| **Client entertainment** | Yes | Client names; company name; business purpose |
| **Home office equipment** | Yes | Must be first purchase — one-time USD 500 limit |

|  | **OUT-OF-POLICY EXPENSES** If you need to submit an expense that exceeds policy limits, get written pre-approval from your manager AND your Finance Business Partner before incurring the expense. Post-hoc approval is not guaranteed. |
