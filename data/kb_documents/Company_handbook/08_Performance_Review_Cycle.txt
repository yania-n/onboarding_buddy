How Performance Management Works at Nexora
Version 1.0   ·   2026   ·   Internal Use Only
Overview
Nexora's performance cycle is designed to be continuous — not a once-a-year event. It combines structured formal reviews with ongoing feedback and real-time recognition.
The Rating Scale
Calibration
After manager ratings are submitted, each department holds a calibration session — a cross-manager discussion to ensure ratings are consistent and fair across teams. The People Analytics team provides benchmarking data. Calibration is facilitated by the HR Business Partner.
Continuous Feedback
How to Give Feedback
Use the Feedback module in Workday to send structured feedback to any colleague at any time
Feedback prompts: 'What went well?', 'What could be even better?', 'What should they keep doing?'
Feedback is visible to the recipient and their manager — there is no anonymous peer feedback at Nexora
NexoraShoutout — Recognition Programme
NexoraShoutout is our peer recognition platform (integrated into Slack). Anyone can give a Shoutout tied to one of our five values. Shoutouts are visible company-wide, and can be redeemed for rewards via the Shoutout portal.
