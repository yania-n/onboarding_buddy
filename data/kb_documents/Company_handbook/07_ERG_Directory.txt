About Our ERGs
Nexora has 18 Employee Resource Groups operating across 40 countries. ERGs are voluntary, employee-led communities that foster belonging, provide professional development, and advise the business on inclusion strategy.
All ERGs are open to all employees — both as members and as allies. You do not need to identify with an ERG's primary focus group to join. ERG membership is tracked in Workday.

Starting or Joining an ERG
How to Join
Go to NexoraConnect > Community > Employee Resource Groups
Find your ERG of interest and click 'Join'
Join the corresponding Slack channel
Attend the next welcome call (scheduled monthly for most ERGs)
How to Start a New ERG
Identify at least 10 founding members across at least 2 regions
Complete the ERG Proposal Template in Confluence: HR > ERG Toolkit
Present to the Inclusion & Belonging team (inclusion@nexoraglobal.com)
Receive People Leadership Committee approval (meets quarterly)
ERG is launched with a dedicated Slack channel, Confluence space, and USD 5,000 annual budget
