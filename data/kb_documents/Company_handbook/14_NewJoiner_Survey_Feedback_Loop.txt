How We Listen and Improve Onboarding

Survey Cadence
Nexora runs three surveys during the onboarding journey. All surveys are delivered via Culture Amp and are confidential. Results are reviewed by the People Analytics team, shared with HR leadership, and fed back into the onboarding programme.
Day 30 Pulse — Question Bank
Standard Questions (all new joiners)
On a scale of 1–10, how supported have you felt in your first 30 days?
My role is matching what was described in the hiring process. (Strongly Agree → Strongly Disagree)
I feel welcome and included by my team. (Strongly Agree → Strongly Disagree)
I have the tools and access I need to do my job. (Yes / Partially / No)
I understand what is expected of me in my first 90 days. (Yes / Somewhat / No)
What one thing would have made your first 30 days better? (Open text)
IT Department-Specific Questions
My system and tool access was provisioned on time. (Yes / Partially / No — with comment)
The technical onboarding (architecture overview, Terraform, Datadog) was at the right level for me. (Too basic / About right / Too advanced)
I understand the on-call process and feel prepared for when I join the rotation. (Yes / Somewhat / No)
Day 90 Onboarding NPS
Net Promoter Score Question
On a scale of 0–10, how likely are you to recommend Nexora's onboarding experience to a friend starting here?
The Feedback Loop
Culture Amp aggregates results weekly
People Analytics team produces monthly onboarding dashboard (shared in HR Leadership review)
Themes from open-text responses reviewed by HR Ops and Dept Onboarding Leads quarterly
Significant issues (e.g. repeated tool access failures, specific team concerns) escalated within 48h
Handbook and process updates made within 4 weeks of a validated issue being identified
Quarterly changelog entry added to the Onboarding System Changelog document
