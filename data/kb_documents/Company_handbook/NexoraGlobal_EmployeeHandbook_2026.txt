2. About Nexora Global Corporation
Our Story
Founded in 2001 by Elena Marchetti and David Kwon, Nexora started as a 12-person software consultancy in San Francisco. Over 25 years we have grown into a global technology and professional services powerhouse operating across five continents.
At a Glance
What We Do
Nexora operates across four core business divisions:
NexoraTech — Enterprise cloud infrastructure, cybersecurity, and AI platforms
NexoraConsult — Strategy, transformation, and managed services for Fortune 500 clients
NexoraData — Advanced analytics, data engineering, and business intelligence solutions
NexoraVentures — Early-stage investment arm focused on B2B SaaS and deep tech
Global Presence
Our key regional hubs include Austin (Global HQ), London (EMEA HQ), Singapore (APAC HQ), and São Paulo (LATAM HQ). Each region has dedicated HR, Finance, Legal, and IT teams supporting local employees while remaining connected to our global operating model.

5. Employment Policies
Equal Opportunity Employment
Nexora is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate on the basis of race, colour, religion, gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, age, national origin, disability, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by applicable law.
Employment Classification
Work Arrangements
Nexora operates a hybrid-first working model. Most roles offer a combination of in-office and remote work. Your arrangement will be specified in your role profile and agreed with your manager. There are three standard arrangements:
Hybrid (default) — Minimum 2 days per week in-office; remainder remote
Office-Based — Role requires primary office presence (e.g., lab, client site)
Remote (approved) — Fully remote; subject to annual manager and HR review
Probationary Period
All new employees serve a 90-day probationary period. During this time, your manager will conduct structured check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days. Successful completion is confirmed in writing via Workday.
Background Checks
Offers of employment are contingent upon satisfactory completion of a background check, including identity verification, right-to-work, employment history, and (for certain roles) criminal record and credit checks, in accordance with local law.
Termination & Resignation
Notice periods are defined in your individual employment contract. Standard notice periods are:
Band 1–3 (Individual Contributor): 4 weeks
Band 4–6 (Manager/Senior): 8 weeks
Band 7+ (Director and above): 12 weeks
6. Compensation & Benefits
Pay Philosophy
Nexora aims to pay at the 65th percentile of the relevant market for all roles. Pay is reviewed annually in Q1, with market benchmarking conducted via Radford and Mercer surveys. Off-cycle adjustments may be made following promotions.
Payroll
Annual Bonus

Equity
Employees at Band 5 and above are eligible for Restricted Stock Unit (RSU) grants. RSUs vest over a 4-year cliff/monthly schedule (25% at year 1, monthly thereafter). Equity is granted upon hire and reviewed at annual performance cycles.
Core Benefits (Global)

Fertility and family-forming support — up to USD 10,000 lifetime benefit
Learning stipend — USD 2,000 per year for external courses, conferences, and certifications
7. Time Off & Leave
Annual Leave
Nexora provides a minimum of 25 days of paid annual leave per year (pro-rated for part-time employees). Some regions provide more in line with local law. Leave must be approved by your manager in Workday.
Public Holidays
Employees observe the public holidays applicable to their country of employment. A full list is available on the NexoraConnect intranet under HR > Holiday Calendar.
Leave Types
Leave Request Process
Submit leave request in Workday at least 5 business days in advance (2 weeks for absences >5 days)
Manager approves or escalates within 3 business days
HR is automatically notified for leaves exceeding 10 consecutive days
Sick leave: Notify your manager before your start time on Day 1 of absence
8. Health, Safety & Wellbeing
Workplace Safety
Nexora is committed to providing a safe, healthy, and hazard-free working environment for all employees, contractors, and visitors. All employees must:
Complete mandatory Health & Safety e-learning within your first 7 days
Report accidents, near-misses, or unsafe conditions to Facilities or your manager immediately
Familiarise yourself with emergency exits, assembly points, and fire procedures at your office location
Mental Health & Wellbeing
Nexora takes mental health seriously. Our global wellbeing programme, NexoraMind, provides:
Free access to Calm and Headspace apps
8 free counselling sessions per year via our EAP partner, Lyra Health
Mental Health First Aiders (MHFAs) trained and available in every office
Quarterly wellbeing webinars covering resilience, sleep, nutrition, and financial health
Ergonomics & Remote Work
Remote and hybrid employees are entitled to a one-time home office setup stipend of USD 500. Ergonomic equipment (desk, chair, monitor) can be requested via Facilities for all office locations. A workstation assessment can be arranged through HR upon request.
9. Code of Conduct & Ethics
Every Nexora employee is required to read, understand, and uphold our Code of Conduct. The full Code is available on NexoraConnect. The key principles are:
Respect & Anti-Harassment
Nexora maintains a zero-tolerance policy for harassment, bullying, discrimination, or any form of workplace misconduct. This applies to all interactions — in-person, virtual, or in writing. Violations will be investigated promptly and may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
Conflicts of Interest
Employees must disclose any personal, financial, or professional relationship that could create a conflict of interest with Nexora's business. Disclosures are made via the Ethics Portal in Workday. When in doubt, disclose.
Anti-Corruption & Bribery
Nexora complies with the US Foreign Corrupt Practices Act, the UK Bribery Act, and all applicable local anti-corruption laws. Employees must never offer, accept, or approve gifts or payments intended to influence business decisions. Gifts valued above USD 50 must be reported.
Whistleblower Protection
Nexora provides a confidential Ethics Hotline (available 24/7) for reporting concerns without fear of retaliation. Reports can be made anonymously. The hotline is operated by an independent third party: 1-800-555-0199 or ethics.nexoraglobal.com.
10. Technology & Data Policy
Acceptable Use
Nexora provides technology tools — including laptops, mobile devices, email, Slack, and cloud services — for business use. Reasonable personal use is permitted provided it does not interfere with work, violate policy, or create security risks.
Do not install unapproved software. All software must be approved via the IT Service Catalogue in ServiceNow.
Do not share your Nexora credentials with anyone, including colleagues.
Lock your screen when stepping away from your workstation.
Use company-approved VPN when accessing Nexora systems remotely.
Data Protection & Privacy
Nexora processes personal data in accordance with GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA (California), and all applicable global privacy laws. Employees who handle personal data must complete mandatory Data Privacy training within their first 10 days.
Employees must never share client, employee, or company confidential data with unauthorised parties. Data classification levels (Public, Internal, Confidential, Restricted) are defined in the Data Governance Policy on NexoraConnect.
AI Tools Policy
The use of generative AI tools (such as ChatGPT, Copilot, or similar) is permitted for productivity purposes but subject to the following:
Never input Nexora Confidential or Restricted data into external AI tools
AI-generated content must be reviewed and validated before use in client deliverables
Approved AI tools are listed in the IT Service Catalogue; use others with prior manager approval

13. Global Mobility & Travel
Business Travel
All business travel must be pre-approved by your manager and booked through Nexora's approved travel management platform, Concur. Nexora's preferred suppliers are listed in the Travel Policy on NexoraConnect.
Expense Reimbursement
Submit expenses in Concur within 30 days of the trip or purchase
All expenses above USD 50 require a receipt
Per diem rates by country are published in the Travel Policy
Business class travel is permitted for flights over 8 hours (Band 6+ only)
International Assignments
Nexora supports short-term assignments (up to 6 months) and long-term international assignments (6 months to 3 years). All assignments are managed by the Global Mobility team in partnership with HR and Legal to ensure appropriate visa, tax, and social security arrangements.
Work-From-Abroad
Employees may work from another country for up to 30 cumulative days per year subject to:
Prior written approval from manager and HR Business Partner
Compliance with local tax and immigration requirements (assessed by Global Mobility)
No client-facing work from jurisdictions where Nexora lacks legal entity
