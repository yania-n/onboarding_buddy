# **Overview**

This matrix defines mandatory training requirements by role type, band, and region. All training is delivered via NexoraLearn (learn.nexoraglobal.com). Completion is tracked in Workday and reported to the Audit & Compliance Committee quarterly.

|  | **NEW JOINERS** All highlighted training marked 'Day 1–30' must be completed within the first 30 days of employment. Managers are notified if a new joiner has not completed mandatory training by day 30. Access to certain systems is gated on completion. |
