# **How to Use This Glossary**

This glossary covers the most common acronyms, internal terms, and technical concepts used at Nexora. It is used by the AI onboarding system to answer 'what does X mean?' questions. If a term is missing, submit it via the glossary update form in Confluence: Company-Wide > Glossary > Submit a Term.

# **A–Z Glossary**

**AE**

Account Executive — quota-carrying sales role responsible for closing enterprise deals

**ARR**

Annual Recurring Revenue — total recurring subscription revenue annualised

**BU**

Business Unit — a distinct division of Nexora (e.g. NexoraTech, NexoraConsult)

**CAB**

Change Advisory Board — IT governance body that approves infrastructure changes

**CLT**

Consolidated Labour Laws (Brazil) — Brazilian federal labour legislation

**CSPM**

Cloud Security Posture Management — continuous monitoring of cloud security configuration

**CSM**

Customer Success Manager — post-sale relationship owner for Nexora customers

**dbt**

Data Build Tool — SQL transformation framework used by the Analytics Platform team

**DoD**

Definition of Done — agreed criteria a story must meet to be considered complete

**DoR**

Definition of Ready — agreed criteria a story must meet before entering a sprint

**DRI**

Directly Responsible Individual — the one person accountable for a decision or deliverable

**EAP**

Employee Assistance Programme — confidential counselling and support service (Lyra Health)

**eNPS**

Employee Net Promoter Score — measures how likely employees are to recommend Nexora

**ERG**

Employee Resource Group — voluntary employee-led community (Nexora has 18)

**FP****&****A**

Financial Planning & Analysis — team that owns budgeting, forecasting, and business partnering

**FPO**

Functional Product Owner — business-side PO who represents user needs and sets feature priorities

**FinOps**

Financial Operations for Cloud — practice of managing cloud costs and spend efficiency

**HRBP**

HR Business Partner — strategic HR professional embedded in or aligned to a business unit

**IaC**

Infrastructure as Code — managing infrastructure via code (Nexora uses Terraform)

**IDP**

Internal Developer Platform — Nexora's self-service tooling for engineering teams

**KPI**

Key Performance Indicator — a measurable metric used to evaluate performance

**KR**

Key Result — the measurable component of an OKR that defines success

**L****&****D**

Learning & Development — the HR function responsible for training and growth

**LGPD**

Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados — Brazil's data protection law (equivalent to GDPR)

**MFA**

Multi-Factor Authentication — mandatory second layer of login verification at Nexora

**NPS**

Net Promoter Score — customer loyalty metric; also used internally (eNPS)

**OKR**

Objectives and Key Results — Nexora's goal-setting framework

**P1/P2/P3/P4**

Incident severity levels: P1 = critical outage; P4 = low-impact cosmetic issue

**PDPA**

Personal Data Protection Act — Singapore's data protection law

**PIR**

Post-Incident Review — blameless retrospective after a P1 or P2 incident

**PO**

Product Owner — person responsible for the product backlog

**PR**

Pull Request — code review mechanism in GitHub before merging changes

**PTU**

Participación de los Trabajadores en las Utilidades — Mexican profit sharing (10% of pre-tax profit)

**RACI**

Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed — decision ownership framework

**SLI**

Service Level Indicator — the actual measurement of a service attribute (e.g. latency)

**SLO**

Service Level Objective — the target value for an SLI (e.g. 99.9% availability)

**SRE**

Site Reliability Engineer — engineering discipline focused on reliability and scalability

**SSO**

Single Sign-On — one login credential for all Nexora systems (Microsoft Azure AD)

**TCP**

Technical Coordination & Planning — the semi-technical team on the Analytics Platform

**TPO**

Technical Product Owner — member of the TCP team who owns the product backlog

**UAT**

User Acceptance Testing — final validation of a feature by business stakeholders

**URG**

Underrepresented Group — employees from groups historically underrepresented in tech/business

**VPC**

Virtual Private Cloud — isolated network environment in AWS

**YoY**

Year on Year — comparison of a metric to the same period in the previous year
