## **How to Submit a Change**

- Edit the relevant source document in Confluence or Workday

- Add an entry to this changelog with the date, document name, change description, priority, and your name

- Notify the People Ops team at people.ops@nexoraglobal.com with subject 'Knowledge Base Update'

- The AI onboarding system will ingest the update in its next weekly refresh (every Monday 6am CT)
