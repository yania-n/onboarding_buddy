# **Purpose**

This document maps every key stakeholder group the IT Department interacts with — who they are, what they need from IT, what IT needs from them, and who the primary relationship owner is. It is used by the onboarding system to answer 'who should I talk to about X?' questions.

# **IT Internal Stakeholders**

| **Stakeholder** | **What they need from IT** | **What IT needs from them** | **IT Relationship Owner** | **Cadence** |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **IT Leadership (Isabella Torres)** | Strategic direction, budget sign-off, escalation decisions | Clear status on risks and delivery; early flags on budget overruns | IT Director | Weekly LT meeting |
| **Analytics Platform Team** | Stable cloud infra, data pipeline reliability, CI/CD pipelines | Architecture requirements, scaling forecasts, security compliance | Cloud Ops Lead | Sprint ceremonies + monthly arch review |
| **Cloud Infrastructure ****&**** Platform Team** | Tooling access, security reviews, cost approvals | Change requests submitted on time, CAB compliance | IT Director | Weekly team sync |
| **Cybersecurity Team** | Cloud security posture data, incident response collaboration | Security requirements for new systems, vulnerability disclosures | Cloud Security Lead | Monthly security review |
| **IT Service Management (ITSM)** | Reliable systems to support, known change schedule | Incident data, change calendar, escalation paths | Cloud Ops Lead | Weekly CAB + incident review |
