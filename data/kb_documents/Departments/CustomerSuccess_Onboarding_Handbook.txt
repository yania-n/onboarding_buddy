1. Welcome to the Customer Onboarding Team
Welcome! You've joined the Customer Onboarding Team — a specialist team within Nexora's Customer Success Department focused entirely on getting new customers to first value as quickly and smoothly as possible.
2. Team Structure & Customer Success Department
Customer Success Department
The Customer Success Department owns the post-sale customer relationship at Nexora — from the moment a contract is signed through to renewal and expansion. It operates across Account Management (retention and growth), Support & Service (issue resolution), and our team — Customer Onboarding (new customer activation).
3. The Onboarding Role — What You Do
Core Responsibilities
Customer onboarding programme management
You own the end-to-end onboarding journey for assigned customers — from the sales handoff call through to go-live sign-off. You manage the project plan, coordinate internal resources (Solutions Engineering, Customer Education), and keep the customer on track.
Technical configuration & integration support
Working with Solutions Engineers, you guide customers through platform configuration, data integration, and user provisioning. You are not responsible for writing code, but you must understand the technical setup well enough to track progress and manage expectations.
Customer training delivery
You deliver or coordinate training sessions for customer admin users and end users. Training follows the Nexora Customer Education playbook — live workshops, e-learning assets, and role-based certification programmes.
Risk identification & escalation
You monitor onboarding health scores and flag at-risk customers early. A customer is 'at risk' if they are more than 5 days behind plan or have an unresolved blocker. Escalate to the Onboarding Lead immediately — never wait for the weekly review.

4. How We Work — Sprint Cadence
The Onboarding Team works in 2-week sprints. Each sprint contains a mix of active customer onboarding project milestones and team improvement work (playbook updates, new training content, process automation).

7. Customer Data & Privacy
Handling Customer Data
Customer data shared during onboarding is strictly confidential — never shared outside the project team without written customer consent
All customer data is stored in Salesforce and the designated SharePoint customer folder — never in personal drives
Customer contact details, contractual terms, and configuration data are classified as Confidential per Nexora's Data Governance Policy
Any suspected data breach involving customer data must be reported to Legal immediately via legal@nexoraglobal.com
8. Definition of Ready & Done
An onboarding project is Ready to start when:
Signed contract is in Salesforce and contract value is confirmed
Sales handoff call completed and discovery notes shared in Salesforce
Success criteria and go-live date agreed with the customer
Internal resources (Solutions Engineer, Customer Education) confirmed available
An onboarding is Done when:
Customer is live on the platform and go-live checklist is signed off by customer
All contracted training sessions delivered and attendance recorded
30-day health score is Green (per CS health score framework)
Formal handoff to CSM completed and documented in Salesforce

11. Your First 30 Days
Week 1 — Orient
Complete Customer Data Privacy and Salesforce training in NexoraLearn
Submit access requests for Salesforce, Gainsight, and Analytics Platform Sandbox
Meet your Onboarding Lead, Solutions Engineers, and onboarding buddy
Review 2 recently completed onboarding projects in Salesforce to understand the journey
Week 2 — Immerse
Shadow your buddy on a live customer kickoff call and weekly check-in
Attend sprint ceremonies and the weekly At-Risk Customer Review
Review the Customer Onboarding Playbook in Confluence end-to-end
Weeks 3–4 — Contribute
Co-manage one active customer onboarding with your buddy
Deliver your first customer training session (with buddy present)
Complete your 30-day check-in with your manager in Workday
