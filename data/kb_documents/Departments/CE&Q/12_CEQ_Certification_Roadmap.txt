About the CCXP
The Certified Customer Experience Professional (CCXP) from the Customer Experience Professionals Association (CXPA) is the most recognised credential in the CX field. It requires 3 years of professional CX experience to sit the exam. Nexora funds the exam fee and recommends CCXP as the target Year 2–3 certification for all CX & NPS team members at Band 3 and above.
