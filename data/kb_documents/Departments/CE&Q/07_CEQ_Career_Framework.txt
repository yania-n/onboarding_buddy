How to Progress in the CX Track
CX and NPS roles at Nexora progress through demonstrated ability to increase the quality and reach of insight — not just volume of output. Key progression indicators:
Your insights are cited in strategic decisions, not just acknowledged
You can design a research programme from scratch without direction
You are proactively identifying CX risks before stakeholders ask
Junior team members seek your review before publishing their work
You can present and defend your methodology to a sceptical executive
