CEQ Glossary & Terminology
Key terms used by the Commercial Excellence & Quality department and the CX & NPS team. Used by the AI onboarding system to answer 'what does X mean?' questions.
CSAT
Customer Satisfaction Score — measures satisfaction with a specific interaction on a scale of 1–5 or 1–10
CES
Customer Effort Score — measures how easy it was for a customer to accomplish a task (e.g. resolve a support issue)
CX
Customer Experience — the sum of all interactions and perceptions a customer has with Nexora across their journey
CCXP
Certified Customer Experience Professional — professional certification from the CXPA; the gold standard CX credential
CEQ
Commercial Excellence & Quality — Nexora's department owning sales excellence, CX/NPS, and quality assurance
Closed-loop
The process of following up with Detractor respondents after an NPS survey to understand and resolve their concerns
Cohort analysis
Grouping customers by a shared characteristic (e.g. contract start date, product tier) and tracking NPS trends for that group over time
CXPA
Customer Experience Professionals Association — the global body for CX professionals; awards the CCXP certification
Detractor
An NPS respondent who scores 0–6 — at risk of churn and unlikely to recommend
Dovetail
Research repository tool used by the CEQ team to store and analyse customer interview transcripts and NPS verbatim comments
eNPS
Employee Net Promoter Score — the NPS methodology applied to employee experience; owned by People Analytics, not CEQ
Journey map
A visual representation of a customer's experience across a specific journey (e.g. onboarding) showing touchpoints, emotions, pain points, and moments of truth
JTBD
Jobs-to-be-Done — a research framework that focuses on the outcomes customers are trying to achieve, not just their stated needs
Medallia
Enterprise CX management platform — used by CEQ for deep analytics on strategic accounts
Moment of truth
A touchpoint in the customer journey that has a disproportionate impact on overall CX perception, positively or negatively
NLP
Natural Language Processing — used by the CX team to automatically tag and classify NPS verbatim comments by theme
NPS
Net Promoter Score — a customer loyalty metric calculated as % Promoters minus % Detractors; ranges from -100 to +100
Passive
An NPS respondent who scores 7–8 — satisfied but not enthusiastic; not counted in NPS calculation
Promoter
An NPS respondent who scores 9–10 — loyal and likely to recommend Nexora
Qualtrics
Survey platform used for NPS, CSAT, and CES programmes at Nexora
r-NPS
Relationship NPS — survey sent periodically (quarterly at Nexora) to measure overall loyalty, not tied to a specific event
Survey fatigue
Decline in response rates caused by too-frequent surveying; Nexora limits contacts to one survey per quarter to avoid this
t-NPS
Transactional NPS — survey triggered by a specific event (e.g. onboarding completion, renewal, support case closure)
VoC
Voice of Customer — the practice of systematically capturing and actioning customer feedback across all channels
