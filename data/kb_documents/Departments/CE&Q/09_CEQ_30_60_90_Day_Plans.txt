How to Use These Plans
Review with your manager in Week 1. Progress reviewed at Day 30, 60, and 90 check-ins in Workday. Adjust as needed based on live business priorities.
30/60/90-Day Plan: CX Analyst
Days 1–30: Learn & Orient
Complete GDPR and Qualtrics training in NexoraLearn before accessing any systems
Submit all Day 1 access requests (Qualtrics, Power BI, Salesforce; Snowflake on Day 1 for DG queue)
Review last 2 NPS Business Review decks in Confluence — understand the baseline scores and key themes
Explore the NPS Scorecard dashboard in Power BI — understand what 'normal' looks like
Shadow an NPS data processing cycle with your buddy
Days 31–60: Immerse & Connect
Own one data quality check cycle end-to-end (with buddy support)
Build or enhance one Power BI chart or dashboard component
Contribute one section of the monthly NPS narrative report
Attend your first NPS Business Review — observe how insights are presented and questioned
Days 61–90: Contribute & Impact
Own the monthly NPS report data processing independently (buddy reviews output)
Deliver one ad hoc insight request from brief to stakeholder delivery
Propose one methodology or data quality improvement to the team
Complete 30-day, 60-day, and 90-day check-ins in Workday
30/60/90-Day Plan: NPS Programme Manager
Days 1–30: Learn & Orient
Complete GDPR, Qualtrics, and NPS Methodology training in NexoraLearn
Shadow the full NPS survey cycle from distribution to report (read the current cycle's data daily)
Introduce yourself to every CSM Team Lead — the closed-loop relationship depends on trust
Review the current NPS contact exclusion list and opt-out process in Confluence
Days 31–60: Immerse & Connect
Co-manage the next NPS survey cycle with your buddy (you drive, buddy reviews)
Own one closed-loop follow-up escalation from alert to resolution
Attend a CSM team meeting to understand how NPS alerts land from their perspective
Days 61–90: Contribute & Impact
Run the NPS survey cycle independently
Present one section of the NPS Business Review to the CCO and VP audience
Propose one programme improvement (response rate, closed-loop speed, survey design)
30/60/90-Day Plan: Journey Mapping Specialist
Days 1–30: Learn & Orient
Complete Research Ethics and GDPR training before any customer contact
Read all 4 published journey maps in Miro — understand scope, methodology, and findings
Review 30 Detractor and 30 Promoter verbatim comments from the last NPS cycle
Shadow a customer interview session with your buddy (observe only)
Days 31–60: Immerse & Connect
Conduct your first 3 customer interviews independently (buddy reviews discussion guide beforehand)
Co-facilitate a synthesis workshop with your buddy for an in-progress journey study
Contribute one section to the next journey map update
Days 61–90: Contribute & Impact
Lead a full mini-journey study from brief to published map (one touchpoint in scope)
Present findings at a CX Leadership Forum or stakeholder session
Propose one journey improvement recommendation backed by your research
