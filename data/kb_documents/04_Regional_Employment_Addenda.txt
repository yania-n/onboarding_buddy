# **North America (USA ****&**** Canada)**

## **Key Legal Framework**

- FLSA (USA) — overtime for non-exempt employees at 1.5× rate after 40h/week

- FMLA (USA) — up to 12 weeks unpaid family/medical leave

- ADA (USA) — reasonable accommodation for disabilities

- Human Rights Code (Canada) — protected grounds include race, gender, disability, sexual orientation

- Employment Standards Act (Ontario) — minimum 2 weeks notice after 1 year

## **Benefits Summary**

- Health: Blue Cross Blue Shield / Sun Life (Canada)

- 401(k) / RRSP: Nexora matches 5%

- PTO: 25 days + 11 federal holidays (USA); 25 days + 10 statutory (Canada)

- Parental: 20 weeks primary / 6 weeks secondary (tops up government entitlement)

## **Notice Periods**

- Band 1–3: 4 weeks

- Band 4–6: 8 weeks

- Band 7+: 12 weeks

# **EMEA (UK, EU, Middle East)**

## **Key Legal Framework**

- UK Employment Rights Act 1996 — unfair dismissal rights after 2 years

- GDPR (EU & UK) — data protection rights; right to erasure; data portability

- Working Time Directive (EU) — maximum 48h/week average; minimum 4 weeks paid leave

- DSGVO (Germany) — co-determination rights; works council consultation required

- UAE Labour Law — 30 calendar days annual leave; end-of-service gratuity

## **Benefits Summary**

- Health: AXA / Cigna Global

- Pension: local statutory + Nexora top-up to 5%

- PTO: 25 days minimum (local law may require more)

- Parental: 20 weeks primary (fully paid); local statutory top-up where required

## **Notice Periods**

- UK: 1 week per year of service (statutory); contractual as per bands

- EU: varies by country — see country-specific addendum in NexoraConnect

- UAE: 30 days (< 5 years tenure), 90 days (5+ years)

# **Asia-Pacific (Singapore, Australia, India, Japan)**

## **Key Legal Framework**

- Employment Act (Singapore) — mandatory itemised payslips; overtime protection

- Fair Work Act (Australia) — National Employment Standards; 10 minimum entitlements

- Shops & Establishments Act (India) — state-specific; varies by Karnataka, Maharashtra etc.

- Labour Standards Act (Japan) — 40h/week maximum; 6 days annual leave after 6 months

- Workplace Health & Safety Act (Singapore/Australia) — employer duty of care

## **Benefits Summary**

- Health: Cigna Global / local insurer

- Superannuation (Australia): 11% employer SGC contribution

- PTO: 25 days (or local minimum if higher)

- Parental: 20/6 weeks (Nexora policy) + government entitlement top-up where applicable

## **Notice Periods**

- Singapore: 1 month (< 2 years); 2 months (2–5 years); 3 months (5+ years)

- Australia: 4 weeks (< 3 years); 5 weeks (3+ years); +1 week if 45+ years old

- India: 1 month (statutory); contractual bands apply

- Japan: 30 days advance notice required by Labour Standards Act

# **Latin America (Brazil, Mexico, Colombia)**

## **Key Legal Framework**

- CLT — Consolidação das Leis do Trabalho (Brazil) — 30 days annual leave; 13th salary

- Ley Federal del Trabajo (Mexico) — profit sharing (PTU); mandatory Sunday premium

- Código Sustantivo del Trabajo (Colombia) — 15 business days annual leave; 30-day notice

- FGTS (Brazil) — employer deposits 8% of salary monthly into employee account

## **Benefits Summary**

- Health: local insurer per country (Bradesco Saúde in Brazil; IMSS in Mexico)

- PTO: 25 days Nexora policy (may exceed local minimum)

- 13th salary (Brazil, Mexico): paid December; Nexora pays proportionally on termination

- Meal vouchers (Brazil): mandatory vale refeição

## **Notice Periods**

- Brazil: 30 days + 3 days per year of service (max 90 days)

- Mexico: 30 days

- Colombia: 30 days
