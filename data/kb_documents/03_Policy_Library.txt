# **Data Protection ****&**** Privacy Policy**

## **Scope**

This policy applies to all Nexora employees, contractors, and third parties who process personal data on behalf of Nexora. It covers data collected from employees, customers, suppliers, and job applicants.

## **Key Principles**

- Lawfulness, fairness and transparency — data is only processed with a valid legal basis

- Purpose limitation — data collected for one purpose is not used for another without fresh consent

- Data minimisation — only the minimum necessary data is collected and retained

- Accuracy — data is kept accurate and up to date; errors are corrected promptly

- Storage limitation — data is not kept longer than necessary (see Retention Schedule in Confluence)

- Integrity and confidentiality — data is protected against unauthorised access and accidental loss

## **Employee Obligations**

- Complete annual GDPR training in NexoraLearn (mandatory by 31 January each year)

- Report suspected data breaches to the Data Protection Officer within 24 hours

- Never share personal data with unauthorised third parties

- Use only approved tools for processing personal data (see Tool Inventory)

- Apply data minimisation — don't collect or retain more than you need

|  | **DATA BREACH REPORTING** If you discover or suspect a data breach, email dpo@nexoraglobal.com immediately and call the IT Security Hotline on ext. 5001. Do not attempt to investigate or contain the breach yourself. |

# **Information Security Policy**

## **Acceptable Use**

- Nexora devices are for business use; reasonable personal use is permitted

- Never install unapproved software — use the IT Service Catalogue in ServiceNow

- Lock your screen when leaving your workstation unattended (Windows: Win+L, Mac: Ctrl+Cmd+Q)

- Never share your credentials with anyone, including colleagues or IT support

- Use the Nexora VPN when accessing company systems remotely

## **Password ****&**** Access Standards**

| **Minimum password length** | 16 characters |
| --- | --- |
| **Complexity** | Upper, lower, number, symbol — no dictionary words |
| **MFA** | Mandatory for all Nexora systems — use Microsoft Authenticator |
| **Password manager** | 1Password (provisioned via IT) — use for all work accounts |
| **Credential sharing** | Never permitted — request shared account access via ServiceNow |
| **Password rotation** | Enforced every 180 days for privileged accounts |

## **Data Classification**

| **Classification** | **Definition** | **Handling** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Public** | Information approved for public release | No restrictions — can be shared externally |
| **Internal** | General company information not for public release | Share internally; don't post publicly |
| **Confidential** | Sensitive business, financial, or customer data | Need-to-know; encrypted at rest and in transit |
| **Restricted** | Highest sensitivity — HR data, legal, M&A, credentials | Strict access control; encrypted; logged access |

# **Expenses ****&**** Travel Policy**

## **General Principles**

- All business expenses must be pre-approved by your direct manager

- Receipts are required for all expenses above USD 25

- Expenses must be submitted in Concur within 30 days of the purchase

- Personal credit cards should be used and reimbursed — Nexora corporate cards available for Band 5+

## **Expense Limits by Category**

| **Category** | **Limit** | **Notes** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Meals (client entertainment)** | USD 100 per person | Requires client names in Concur notes |
| **Meals (team working lunch)** | USD 30 per person | Manager approval; note team members |
| **Hotels** | USD 250/night (Tier 1 cities), USD 175/night (others) | Book via Concur travel portal |
| **Flights** | Economy class (< 6h), Business (≥ 6h, Band 6+) | Book 14+ days in advance where possible |
| **Ground transport** | Actual cost | Uber/taxi; rental cars require pre-approval |
| **Home office equipment** | USD 500 one-time stipend | Claimed via Workday Expenses, not Concur |
| **Learning ****&**** conferences** | USD 2,000/year | Requires manager + HR BP approval in Workday |

|  | **ANTI-BRIBERY** Never claim expenses for gifts to clients or officials above USD 50 in value. Any gift above this threshold must be declared in the Ethics Portal in Workday regardless of who pays. |

# **Code of Conduct Summary**

The full Code of Conduct is available in NexoraConnect. All employees must read and attest annually. This section summarises the key obligations.

## **Core Obligations**

| **Principle** | **What it means** | **Where to get help** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Integrity** | Act honestly in all dealings; never misrepresent facts or conceal information | Your manager or Ethics Hotline |
| **Respect** | Treat all colleagues, customers and partners with dignity; zero tolerance for harassment | HR Business Partner |
| **Confidentiality** | Protect Nexora's confidential information and that of our clients and partners | Legal & Compliance |
| **Conflict of Interest** | Disclose any personal interest that may conflict with Nexora's interests | Ethics Portal in Workday |
| **Anti-Corruption** | Never offer or accept bribes, kickbacks or improper gifts | Legal or Ethics Hotline |
| **Fair Competition** | Never discuss pricing, strategy or competitive plans with competitors | Legal & Compliance |
| **Sustainability** | Act in line with Nexora's 2030 Net-Zero commitment in all decisions | Sustainability team |

## **Ethics Hotline**

Reports can be made confidentially 24/7 at ethics.nexoraglobal.com or 1-800-555-0199. Reports can be anonymous. Nexora prohibits retaliation against anyone who raises a genuine concern in good faith.
